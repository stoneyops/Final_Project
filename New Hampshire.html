The state of New Hampshire is located in the Northeastern United States' New England area. It shares borders with the Canadian provinces of Quebec to the north, Massachusetts to the south, Vermont to the west, Maine and the Gulf of Maine to the east, and Massachusetts to the south. With little more than 1.3 million inhabitants, New Hampshire ranks as the tenth least populated and fifth-smallest state in terms of area among the 50 U.S. states.
